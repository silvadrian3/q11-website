About the Product

A “cloud” based Roadside Assistance application, designed for a worry-free easy access “at Your Fingertip”. It conveniently utilizes your mobile phone’s GPS capabilities to pinpoint your exact location, allowing immediate dispatch of our service providers for emergency assistance, even if you don’t know where you are. Q11 GPS Assist protects and ensures peace- of-mind and security while on the road helping vehicle owners, when it matters most.

Integrate roadside assistance service to your mobile lifestyle, available for “FREE”. The application provides owners with roadside and non-roadside services for emergency assistance such as accidents, battery, tire, fuel, lockout and more. In addition to providing drivers assistance, Q11 GPS Assist reduces wait or hold time while maintaining quick response time and efficient communication between customers and service providers. In addition to giving drivers one-touch access easy access for emergencies, it eliminates the need for a troubled motorist to relay information during a stressful situation.
